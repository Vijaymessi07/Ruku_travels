About Us

Your Trusted
Travel Partner

Rukku Travels is a travel service in Dindigul.
We started our service on 01/01/2025 with a goal to give good travel experience.
We provide travel service all over Tamil Nadu.
We have completed more than 100+ trips successfully.
We have more than 273+ happy customers who trust our service.

We provide all types of vehicles for every travel need.
We have cars, tempo travellers, and vehicles for small and big groups.
Our vehicles are clean, comfortable, and well maintained.
We also provide new vehicles with classic models for better experience.

Our drivers are friendly, experienced, and drive safely.
We make sure every journey is smooth and without problems.
We help customers plan their trips easily.
We support family trips, group trips, and tourist trips.

We also provide trips to popular tourist places in Tamil Nadu.
We have a cottage available in Kodaikanal for a peaceful stay.
Customers can enjoy both travel and stay with our service.

We are always ready to support our customers anytime.
We focus on safety, comfort, and customer satisfaction.
Our aim is to give safe, easy, and happy travel for everyone.
